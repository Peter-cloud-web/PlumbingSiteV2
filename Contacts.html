Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and processes

How quickly can you respond to emergency calls?

We aim to respond to emergency calls within 30-60 minutes during business hours and within 2 hours for after-hours emergencies. Our rapid response team is strategically positioned to serve the greater Ruiru and Kiambu areas efficiently.

Do you provide free consultations and quotes?

Yes, we offer free consultations for all our services including borehole drilling, swimming pool construction, and water treatment systems. Our experienced team will assess your needs and provide detailed, transparent quotes with no hidden costs.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Absolutely. All our technicians are fully licensed, certified, and insured. We maintain comprehensive liability insurance and ensure all our team members meet the highest professional standards and safety requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including cash, bank transfers, mobile money (M-Pesa), and checks. For larger projects, we offer flexible payment terms and can discuss installment plans to suit your budget requirements.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. Standard plumbing work comes with a 1-year warranty, while major installations like boreholes and swimming pools include extended warranties. Specific warranty terms are provided with each service quote.
