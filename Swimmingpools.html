Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our swimming pool services

How long does it take to build a swimming pool?

The timeline for constructing a swimming pool typically ranges from 8-12 weeks, depending on factors such as pool size, design complexity, weather conditions, and any additional features like landscaping or decking. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project.

What types of swimming pools do you construct?

We offer construction services for various pool types including concrete/gunite pools, vinyl liner pools, and fiberglass pools. We also specialize in custom designs such as infinity edge pools, lap pools, natural pools, and recreational pools for both residential and commercial properties.

How often should I service my pool equipment?

Pool equipment should be professionally serviced at least once every 3-6 months to ensure optimal performance and longevity. Regular maintenance helps identify potential issues before they become major problems. We offer customized maintenance plans based on your pool's specific equipment and usage patterns.

What chemicals are essential for pool maintenance?

Essential pool chemicals include chlorine or other sanitizers, pH adjusters (acids and bases), alkalinity increasers, calcium hardness adjusters, and algaecides. Our maintenance team handles chemical balancing professionally, ensuring your pool water remains clear, safe, and properly balanced at all times.

What permits are required for pool construction?

Swimming pool construction typically requires building permits, electrical permits, and possibly environmental permits depending on your location. As part of our service, we handle the entire permitting process including submission of plans, coordination with inspectors, and ensuring all regulatory requirements are met.
